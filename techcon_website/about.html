History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of local developers and tech enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. What started as a weekend meetup for 50 passionate individuals has grown into one of the world's premier technology conferences, attracting over 10,000 attendees from across the globe.

Over the years, TechCon has evolved from focusing primarily on web development to encompassing artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, blockchain, and emerging technologies. Our conference has been at the forefront of technological innovation, providing a platform where breakthrough ideas are shared and collaborative partnerships are formed.

Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and practical implementation. TechCon 2024 aims to foster innovation, facilitate knowledge sharing, and create meaningful connections within the global tech community.

We believe that technology has the power to solve humanity's greatest challenges. Through our conference, we strive to bring together brilliant minds, provide educational opportunities, and inspire the next generation of tech leaders to build a better, more connected world.

Past Speakers

TechCon has been honored to host some of the most influential voices in technology. Here are some of our notable past speakers who have shared their insights and vision:

Dr. Sarah Chen

Former Director of AI Ethics at Google and current Professor at Stanford University. Dr. Chen's groundbreaking work in responsible AI development has shaped industry standards and government policies worldwide.

Marcus Rodriguez

Founder and CEO of SecureTech Solutions, Marcus is a renowned cybersecurity expert who has helped Fortune 500 companies defend against cyber threats. His keynote on "The Future of Digital Security" was one of our most attended sessions.

Lisa Park

Blockchain pioneer and lead developer of the EcoChain protocol. Lisa's work in sustainable cryptocurrency mining has revolutionized how we think about blockchain's environmental impact.

Dr. James Wilson

Senior Quantum Computing Researcher at IBM and author of "Quantum Leap: The Future of Computing." Dr. Wilson's presentations on quantum algorithms have inspired countless developers to explore this emerging field.
